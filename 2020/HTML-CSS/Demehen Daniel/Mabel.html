DSC:

Developer Students Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google Developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joinimg a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community

What we offer you:

  • Game Development
  • Mobile Development
  • Frontend web Development
  • Python
  • UI
  • Cyber security
